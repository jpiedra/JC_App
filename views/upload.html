Upload a level for use in JavaCrypt

JavaCrypt levels follow a format of 12 rows containing 12 tiles (at 48 pixels height/width each).

This regular format makes uploading levels to use in-game easy to do.

Instructions

The following characters will be processed as wall blocks: O, o

The following character will be processed as empty, traversable space: -

When you have entered 12 such characters for every row, the level plan is complete.

You can press 'Submit Level' below to submit the plan.
